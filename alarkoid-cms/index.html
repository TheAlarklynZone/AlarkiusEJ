The World of Hibryth

A continent shaped by three great wars, six ruling houses, and one unbroken chain of prophecy stretching back to the Age of Founding. The land is alive with old magic — not the kind that can be conjured, but the kind that remembers.

The Six Houses

Each house rules a region of Hibryth and claims descent from one of the six original gods. Their crests, colors, and mottos are woven into the legal, spiritual, and military fabric of the realm. No house has ever stood alone — and none has ever fully trusted another.
